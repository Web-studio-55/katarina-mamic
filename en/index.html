A story that began
with a single heart

It all started with a heart-adorned tunic — created spontaneously, sold out in an instant.

Heart-motif dresses in matching mom-and-daughter sets followed, defining nearly a decade. Today the focus is on occasion dresses: over 30 styles, each designed and tailored in our atelier in the heart of Zagreb.
