Shipping & Returns

Last updated: July 2026

Shipping

  • Orders are shipped via courier (GLS / Croatian Post) across Croatia.
  • Delivery time: 2–5 business days from order confirmation for in-stock styles. Made-to-measure dresses: up to 10 business days.
  • Shipping cost: €5 for orders up to €100. Orders over €100 — free shipping.
  • EU delivery: €15, delivery within 5–10 business days.
  • Personal pickup at our atelier (Gundulićeva 23, Zagreb) — free, by appointment.

Returns & exchanges

You have the right to withdraw from the purchase within 14 days of receiving the product, without stating a reason, in accordance with EU consumer protection law.

  • The product must be unworn, undamaged, with original tags and packaging.
  • Notify us at katarina.racuni@gmail.com — we will send you instructions and a withdrawal form.
  • Refunds are issued within 14 days of receiving the returned product, using the original payment method.
  • Direct return shipping costs are borne by the customer.
  • Size exchange: possible within the same period — contact us and we will reserve your size.
Exception: dresses made or altered to the customer’s measurements are excluded from the right of withdrawal, as they are goods made to the consumer’s specifications.

Complaints

If a product has a material defect, contact us with a photo and description at katarina.racuni@gmail.com. We resolve complaints as quickly as possible, within the statutory 15-day period.
